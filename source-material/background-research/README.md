# Background Research — Carrier-Portal Credential Management

This folder preserves the in-depth research conducted to validate the problem
Alex Metka (Brown & Brown, Central Florida) raised on the **2026-06-12** discovery
call, and to assess the competitive and regulatory landscape for DataBraid.

## What's here

| File | Contents |
|---|---|
| `findings.md` | The human-readable analysis: problem validation, existing tooling, and red flags. **Start here.** |
| `verified-claims.md` | All claims that **survived** adversarial verification, grouped by theme, with source quality + date. |
| `refuted-claims.md` | Claims that were **refuted** during verification — quarantined so weak/marketing numbers are never reused. |
| `sources.md` | The 32 source URLs gathered across the search sweeps, grouped by topic. |

## How it was produced

Generated by the `deep-research` workflow (run ID `wf_30743b67-2ce`) on 2026-06-18.
The harness fanned out 6 parallel web-search sweeps across the three research
angles, fetched and mined **52 sources into 219 candidate claims**, then ran
**122 adversarial verification votes** (a claim is killed only on a 2-of-3
refute majority).

**The run was stopped before the final synthesis stage**, so `findings.md` was
synthesized by hand directly from the cached, verified claims in the workflow
journal — not auto-generated. Counts:

- **96 of 122** verified claims **survived** adversarial fact-checking
- **26** were **refuted** — almost entirely vendor/advocacy marketing figures
  (chiefly ID Federation "SignOn Once" promotional content and Ivans product
  copy). These are isolated in `refuted-claims.md`.

## Reliability guidance

- **Trust and quote freely:** the 2024 ID Federation *survey* data (via Insurance
  Journal), NYDFS Part 500 regulatory text, and court-case analyses (Morgan
  Lewis / Proskauer / Greenberg Traurig / Steptoe).
- **Do NOT quote externally without independent confirmation:** any cost or
  help-desk figure originating from a vendor with a product to sell
  (ID Federation, Ivans, individual portal-automation blogs). See
  `refuted-claims.md` for the specific numbers that failed verification.

## Research scope (the three questions asked)

1. **Problem validation beyond Brown & Brown** — is carrier-portal credential /
   access lifecycle management a widespread, documented industry pain?
2. **Existing tooling** — insurance-specific (AMS, comparative raters, carrier
   SSO) and generic (password managers, IAM, RPA), and what each does / does not
   solve; plus carrier-side identity standards (Ivans, ACORD, NIPR).
3. **Red flags / risks** — carrier ToS, MFA/bot-detection, credential-storage
   liability, legal precedent (CFAA, hiQ v. LinkedIn), insurance regulation
   (GLBA, NYDFS 500), and what has killed similar aggregator/vault plays before.
